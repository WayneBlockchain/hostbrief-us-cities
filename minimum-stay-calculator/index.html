Straight about what this is
Arithmetic, not a forecast

This tool is deliberately narrow so you can trust it. It computes a threshold; you supply the judgment. Here's exactly what it does and doesn't tell you.

  • It does not predict your bookings. It claims nothing about occupancy, demand, seasonality or what will happen — only the most you could afford to lose before the move stops paying.
  • It's a break-even line, not advice. Whether raising your minimum will actually cost you fewer bookings than the threshold is your call, based on your own market.
  • The fee applies to your cleaning fee too. That's why the real cost of a turnover uses the 15.5% host-only fee — the cleaning fee you charge doesn't reach you whole.
  • Cleans saved are shown at your current occupancy — the concrete workload change — not at the break-even occupancy. It's the drop in turnovers you'd feel if demand held.
  • Every figure is rounded to the nearest whole unit for readability.
