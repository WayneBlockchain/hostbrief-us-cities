Straight about the data
No black box

This index is built so a host can trust it. Here's exactly what's behind the numbers — and what they can and can't tell you.

  • Source: Inside Airbnb — public-domain listing data.
  • Snapshot: June 2026. STR pricing shifts seasonally, so treat it as a recent baseline, not live.
  • What we compute: nightly price, listing counts and the area & bedroom groupings — straight from Inside Airbnb's public listings.
  • Occupancy & revenue are Inside Airbnb's own published estimates (their estimated_occupancy and estimated_revenue, trailing 12 months) — not a HostBrief model, and not hosts' real dashboards. Good for comparison, not accounting.
  • Suburbs assigned by map location, so "Sea Point" means actually-in-Sea-Point.
  • Medians, not averages — one R50k/night villa won't skew your area.
