PREFRONTAL CORTEX.

Tilt Shield™ ™

NeuralFeed™ ™
Priority alert system
Intelligent alert pill above the strip. Only fires on threshold crossings — never spams. Short stack, heater detected, push/fold mode, tilt collapse — each with a clear action and clickable response.
Stake Transition Guard™ ™
Bankroll protection
Watches your buy-in count in real time. Tells you when you're ready to move up — and when you must move down immediately. No ego. Just math. Your bankroll survives because of it.

Street-by-street fold tracking across sessions. Detects slow-developing leaks like river over-folding or turn aggression drops that PT4 can't show you until you've lost 100 buy-ins.
