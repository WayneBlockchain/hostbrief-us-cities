This index is built so a host can trust it. Here's exactly what's behind the numbers — and what they can and can't tell you.

  • Source: Inside Airbnb — public-domain listing data.
  • Snapshot: November 2025. Short-term rental pricing shifts seasonally, so treat it as a recent baseline, not live.
  • Revenue & occupancy are estimates from a review-based model, not hosts' real dashboards. Good for comparison, not accounting.
  • Council-district data was remapped to recognisable neighbourhoods by listing location.
  • Medians, not averages — one ultra-luxury outlier won't skew your area.
